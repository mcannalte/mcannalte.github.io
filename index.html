Student

I am currently pursuing a BS in computer science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My course work is split ~equally between topics in software engineering and data science.


There, I've been an inaugural Engineering City Scholar and a fellow of the 3rd cohort of the iVenture Accelerator. My time outside of the classroom is mostly directed toward entrepreneurship, research, and side projects.

Entrepreneur

I got my start as an entrepreneur by co-founding a crowdfunding startup called Bilder engineered for non-profits and local governments. The platform I helped build is no longer around, although it has been a compelling foray into further pursuits.


My interests today lie in electronic voting, brain-computer interfaces, and scalable methods of crowdsourcing.

Professional

As a professional, I have best excelled in a rapid-prototyping environment. Given a set of constraints, I enjoy developing creative solutions with the foresight to scale in production.


I have worked as both a data scientist and a software engineer, with preference only given to the role whose problems require the most compelling solutions.
