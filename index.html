What I Do

I am a student pursuing my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science at the University of Illinois -- Urbana-Champaign. I come from Lake in the Hills, IL, where I am an alumnus of Huntley High School.

I am also a developer and core team member at Bilder, an institutional crowdfunding platform, student startup, and object of my sincerest pride. Entrepreneurship is my passion.
